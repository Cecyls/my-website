Curriculum & info

In 2012 I began my studies in Graphic Design at the Academy of Fine Arts in Naples and immediately became interested in the editorial and product design world.
In 2015 I participated in the national design competition “Scenari di Innovazione 11”, organized by Artex, presenting a cardboard frame loom, which won the 2nd prize.
After the bachelor's degree, I continued my studies in Graphic Design for public communication and participated at the wayfinding system design for the Certosa di San Martino museum (Naples).
I completed my master's studies with a thesis about the experimental book design and typography.
The degree thesis project was exhibited at the Aiap Book Community at the event WE AIAP, held in Milan in November 2018.
Winner of the second edition of the competition “I libri-gioco si fanno con le macchine”, announced by Ts'ai Lun 105 association, I carried out an internship at Cartotecnica Montebello S.p.A. at the beginning of 2019.
I consider myself flexible and motivated, always trying to improve my professional skills. Over the years I have developed the ability to work in teams, trying to build positive relationships.

Graphic Design

I graduated in Graphic Design at the Academy of Fine Arts in Naples, with a mark of 110 cum laude and praise from the commission. During my professional career at communication agencies I gained experience in brand identity, logo design, packaging, layout.

Product Design

In 2015 I designed a cardboard loom, in collaboration with Marco De Luca, which was presented at the national design competition "Scenari di Innovazione 11", organized by Artex. The project won the 2nd prize and was presented at the 29th edition of Futuro Remoto in Naples. I am particularly passionate about product design in the paper industry, thanks also to the internship at Cartotecnica Montebello (Vicenza), leader in the book-game market.

Book Design

During the preparatory research for the writing of my specialist thesis entitled "The forms of book. Experimental and unconventional typography" I had the opportunity to treat at 360° the unconventional typography and book design. From this research, I made the project "Experimental novel: La Scomparsa di Patò", presented at the WE AIAP event. I won the second edition of the competition "The game-books are made with machines", launched by the association Ts'ai Lun 105, thanks to which I did an internship at Cartotecnica Montebello (Vicenza). After the internship I realized the book game "First books with wires". Both my projects have been selected for the exhibition at the Oggetto Libro 2020 festival.

Motion Design

In the last few years I've also become passionate about motion graphics and I made the music video "Luna" by Geiro taking care of every aspect, from concept to animation.
